Scrambled Eggs Special - Odin's Recipes

Have a heavenly and tastefull experience right in your kitchen!
Cheap & easy to prepare - no pro skills needed.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of black peper
  • Parsley Leaves

Directions

  1. Put all the ingredients together in a bowl;
  2. Whisk it until completely combined and very foamy;
  3. Fry it in a frying pan or any pan you have;
  4. Remove from the pan an enjoy it!!!
