Instant Lamen - Odin's Recipes

Bring exotic flavours to your kitchen without spending lots of money!
Cheap & easy to prepare - no pro skills needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 pac of Instant Lamen of your choice (Seasoning Included)
  • 500 ml of boiling water

Directions

  1. Put the Lamen inside the boiling water;
  2. Cook until it gets soft;
  3. Add the seasoning powder;
  4. Mix it with a fork;
  5. Put it in a bowl and Enjoy It!!!
