Bologna Sandwich - Odin's Recipes

The most delicius sandwich on earth, healthy and tasteful!
Cheap & easy to prepare - no pro skills needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bread
  • 200g of Sliced Bologna
  • Mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Cut the bread in half;
  2. Put lots of mayo on both halves of bread;
  3. Fill it with the Bologna Slices - The more the better!;
  4. Enjoy it!!!
